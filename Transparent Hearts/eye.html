Eye Awareness Campaign

Eyes are the most precious gifts which humans possess. It lets us explore the world around us.
The probable reasons could be the increase in the use of mobile phones, tablets, laptops and TVs. Children spend a major part of their day using these electronic gadgets which harm their eyes badly. Mother reason could be that children eat junk food more than healthy food. They are reluctant to eat green vegetables which are actually beneficial for healthy eyesight.

  • We should have in a balanced diet containing green leafy vegetables and eatables rich n vitamin A, C and E, Omega-3 add zinc.
  • Use sunglasses of right kind that block UVA and UVB rays.
  • Avoid staring at PCs, TVs, phones for long.
  • Give rest to your eyes by looking 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
  • While using these gadgets, get up and take a 15-minute break after every 2 hours.
  • When you feel changes or weakness in your eyesight, consult an ophthalmologist and if some problem occurs, visit him frequently for check-ups.
