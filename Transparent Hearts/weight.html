Aim for light Weight

Gaining weight is a common problem faced by many individuals.
Being overweight or obese can lead to a number of health problems such as heart disease, diabetes, and high blood pressure.
Additionally, reducing stress levels and getting enough sleep can also play a role in preventing weight gain.
It's important to remember that everyone's body is different and what works for one person may not work for another. Finding a healthy diet and exercise routine that works for you can go a long way in preventing weight gain and maintaining a healthy weight.
Finally, it's important to have a positive mindset and be patient with yourself. Rapid weight loss is not sustainable and slow, steady progress is the key to long-term success.Gaining weight can be prevented by:

  • Avoiding junk
  • Sugary drinks
  • Fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Regular exercise
  • Hitting the gym
  • Regular Weight Checks
