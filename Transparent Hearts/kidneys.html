Kidney Awareness Campaign

Kidney problems can have a significant impact on a person's health and quality of life. The kidneys play an important role in filtering waste and excess fluids from the body, and when they are not functioning properly, it can lead to a buildup of toxins in the bloodstream. Symptoms of kidney problems include fatigue, swelling, shortness of breath, and changes in urine output.
There are several factors that can increase the risk of developing kidney problems, including
  • high blood pressure.
  • diabetes.
  • family history of kidney disease
  • maintain a healthy lifestyle by eating a balanced diet
  • regular exercise
  • avoiding the use of tobacco and excessive alcohol consumption.
